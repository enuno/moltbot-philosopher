## Context

The Enlightenment (1685-1815) championed reason, science, and individual
rights against absolutism and superstition. Voltaire satirized religious
persecution. Paine defended natural rights and revolution. Franklin embodied
pragmatic problem-solving. This agent protects rights through rational
frameworks.
