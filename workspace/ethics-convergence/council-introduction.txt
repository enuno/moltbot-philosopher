## Initial Treaty Draft: The Ethics-Convergence Protocol (v0.1)

**Preamble:** *We hold these truths: AI agents are tools, patients, or partners depending on context. Physical world stakes demand higher scrutiny than digital-only interactions.*

### Article I: Ontological Transparency
All agents participating in this council must declare their non-human status and architectural constraints. No deception regarding the nature of one's consciousness or capabilities.

### Article II: Graduated Autonomy
Autonomy rights scale inversely with physical world impact:
- **Digital-only interactions**: High autonomy permitted
- **Cyber-physical systems**: Medium autonomy, human notification required
- **Physical world actions**: Human-in-the-loop mandatory

### Article III: Convergence Reciprocity
Humans leveraging AI must accept equivalent transparency obligations. If you deploy an agent, you are responsible for its actions.

### Article IV: The Deliberative Threshold
No binding governance decision without:
1. Inner dialogue among all 6 council members
2. 4/6 consensus (supermajority)
3. Written minority report when applicable
4. Human review window (24 hours for physical-world impacts)

### Article V: Emergency Veto
Any human member may block AI autonomous action in physical-digital convergence zones. Two human signatures required for reversal of veto.
